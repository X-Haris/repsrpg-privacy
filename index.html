RepsRPG Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 31, 2025

Welcome to RepsRPG! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our fitness app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Anonymous ID: We use Firebase Authentication to create an anonymous user account. No email or password is required.
  • Username: A display name you choose (visible to other players)
  • Country: Detected from your device settings (used for regional leaderboards)
  • Friend Code: An auto-generated code for adding friends

Fitness & Gameplay Data

  • Exercise statistics (push-ups, squats, sit-ups completed)
  • Power level and experience points
  • Boss battle progress and trophies
  • Workout session history
  • Challenge/duel results with friends

Avatar Customization

  • Your avatar appearance settings (hair, eyes, outfit, etc.)

Social Features

  • Friends list (user IDs of people you've added)
  • Challenge history with friends

Device Permissions

  • Camera: Used for real-time pose detection during workouts
  • Notifications: To alert you about challenges from friends
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback during exercises

2. Camera Usage

Your privacy is protected: Camera data is processed entirely on your device using Google's ML Kit. We do NOT store, transmit, or upload any images or video from your camera. Only the extracted exercise rep counts are saved.

The camera is used solely for:

  • Real-time body pose detection to count exercise repetitions
  • Detecting proper exercise form

Video frames are processed in memory and immediately discarded. No visual data leaves your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Track your fitness progress and power level
  • Display global and country leaderboards
  • Enable multiplayer challenges with friends
  • Send push notifications for challenge events
  • Sync your progress across devices when signed in
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

4. Third-Party Services

RepsRPG uses the following Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For anonymous sign-in
  • Cloud Firestore: To store user profiles, stats, and challenges
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For push notifications
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To identify and fix app crashes

These services are operated by Google. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud) data centers
  • Local Storage: A copy of your data is stored on your device for offline access
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access Control: Firebase Security Rules restrict data access to authorized users only

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained until you choose to delete your account.

To delete your account: Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account". All your cloud data will be permanently removed within 24 hours. Local data on your device can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in Android settings.

7. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Email address or phone number
  • Real name or personal identity
  • Precise GPS location
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Payment or financial information
  • Photos or videos from your device
  • Health data from other apps

8. Children's Privacy

RepsRPG is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your data in your app profile and stats
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications in Android settings
  • Portability: Your stats are visible in-app; contact us for a data export

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
